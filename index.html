Who Let Us Have a Website?

Look, we're not professional analysts (obviously). We're the folks yelling at the TV, celebrating miracle catches, and debating questionable calls over cheap beer and lukewarm wings. Koozie Sports is basically that group chat that got way out of hand.

We started this to share our passionate (read: biased) takes on everything from the NFL gridiron to the local pub league standings. Expect hot takes, cold beers, and maybe an occasional coherent thought.

Our Philosophy: Keep it real, keep it fun, and don't spill your drink.
